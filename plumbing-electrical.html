Plumbing — Minor Jobs

  • Fixing leaking taps, traps & wastes
  • Replacing flexi hoses & isolation valves
  • Unblocking sinks, basins & showers
  • Toilet repairs (fill/flush valves, seats, seals)
  • Siliconing baths, showers & sinks
  • Installing washing machines & dishwashers
  • Radiator valve swaps & bleeding (no system drain‑downs)

No gas: I do not work on boilers, cookers, gas fires, or any gas‑related appliances/pipework.

Electrical — Minor Jobs (Non‑Notifiable)

  • Replace sockets, switches & spurs
  • Light fittings, pendants & lamps
  • Extractor fans like‑for‑like swaps
  • Appliance connection (hard‑wire hobs/ovens if suitable isolator exists)
  • Fault finding on simple circuits (RCD trips, loose terminations)
  • Replace/fit doorbells, thermostats (non‑smart rewires)
  • Minor repairs to damaged cables/accessories

Domestic non‑notifiable works only (Part P). For consumer unit changes, new circuits or extensive rewires I’ll refer a certified electrician.
