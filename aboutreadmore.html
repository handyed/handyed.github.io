About Me

From a young age, I was drawn to the art of building and fixing. My father, a skilled mechanic, was my greatest inspiration. While he always told me to go and play, I couldn’t help but watch in awe as he worked—his hands were like magic, cold yet capable of creating wonders. I wanted to be just like him.

Over the years, I tried many different jobs, but I always found myself coming back to what I loved: working with my hands. Whether it’s repairing, building, or maintaining, I know I can finish any task with skill and precision because it's in my blood.

My father, the best mechanic I ever knew, instilled in me the drive to never settle for less than the best. His memory guides me, and it’s because of the love and passion I have for what I do that I’m able to deliver the highest quality in every job I take on.

I’m not just fixing things; I’m continuing a legacy of craftsmanship—one that I am proud to carry forward every day.
