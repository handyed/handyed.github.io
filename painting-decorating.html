Painting & Decorating Services

At Handy Ed, we offer professional painting and decorating services for both residential and commercial spaces. Whether you're refreshing a single room or undertaking a full property renovation, we provide expert services to make your space look its best. We are proud to use VOC-free paints that are safe for sensitive individuals, including children and those with asthma or other respiratory conditions.

Our Painting & Decorating Services Include:

  • Interior painting (walls, ceilings, trim, etc.)
  • Exterior painting and touch-ups
  • Wallpaper installation and removal
  • Decorative finishes and techniques
  • Cabinet and furniture painting
  • Colour consultations and design advice
  • Plastering and wall repairs

Why Choose Us?

  • Use of VOC-free, low-odor paints for a healthier environment
  • Safe for sensitive people, including children and those with asthma
  • Experienced and skilled painters and decorators
  • Timely, clean, and professional service
  • Affordable pricing with attention to detail
