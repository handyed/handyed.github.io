Introduction At Handy Ed, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the ways in which we collect, use, protect, and manage your personal data when you access our services or interact with our website. We recognize the importance of protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal information.
1. Information We Collect We collect personal information to provide and improve our services, as well as to keep you informed. The types of information we may collect include:
  • Personal Details: Name, address, email address, phone number, and payment information.
  • Transaction Data: Details of the services you purchase, including payment history and billing details.
  • Communication Data: Information provided through any correspondence with us, such as emails or messages submitted via our website.
  • Usage Data: Data on how you use our website and services, including IP address, device type, browser, and usage patterns.
2. How We Use Your Information We utilize your personal information for several purposes, including but not limited to:
  • Service Provision: To provide, manage, and improve the services you request.

  • Legal and Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, including to enforce our terms of service or respond to lawful requests from authorities.
3. Sharing of Information We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following situations:

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose personal information if required by law or when we believe such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or respond to lawful requests from government authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business, your personal information may be transferred as part of the transaction.
4. Data Security We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

  • Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Erasure: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain conditions and legal obligations.
  • Restriction of Processing: You can request that we limit the use of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Data Portability: In some cases, you may request that we transfer your personal data to another service provider.
  • Opt-Out: You have the option to opt-out of marketing communications and newsletters at any time.
