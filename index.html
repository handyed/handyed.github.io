Our services

Plumbing

Repairs & Installations

Install, repair, or replace taps, sinks, toilets, baths, showers.
Replace or install pipework (copper, plastic, etc.) inside the property.
Fit or replace radiators (as long as you’re not touching a gas boiler).
Unblock or repair waste pipes & drains.
Install washing machines, dishwashers (water connection only).
Work on cold water systems (tanks, storage, supply).

Electrical

Electrical Work

Replacing accessories like light switches, sockets, ceiling roses.
Replacing a fused connection unit or plug top.
Replacing or repairing a damaged cable in the same position.
Installing or replacing a light fitting.
Adding a spur from an existing ring or radial circuit (outside special locations).
Minor repairs and maintenance, like changing a lampholder, fuse, or MCB (same rating).
